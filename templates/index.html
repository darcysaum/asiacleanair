Our Air. Our Asia.

About the Project

Unparalleled economic development in Asia during the past few decades has also led to increases in air pollutants and greenhouse gases—as a consequence, Asian cities suffer some of the worst air pollution in the world. This situation is expected to worsen in the future, threatening to contribute to climate change and endanger the health of the 1.8 billion people who live in Asia’s urban centers.

Our goal with "Our Air, Our Asia" is to create a tool to give people easy access to information about air quality in their cities. We also hope that our website can provide a bigger picture on the severity of air pollution in cities across Asia.
