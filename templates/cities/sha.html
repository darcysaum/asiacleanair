Population: 19.2 million (2009 figures)

According to media reports, Shanghai’s air quality has plummeted since the end of the World Expo in October 2010. At one point in November 2010, the city recorded an API of 370—the worst reading in ten years. The recent decline in Shanghai’s air quality is in part due to heavy local construction as well as the restarting of hay burning and less frequent road cleaning after the end of the Expo. Moreover, dust brought by sandstorms in northern areas is also exacerbating the problem.
