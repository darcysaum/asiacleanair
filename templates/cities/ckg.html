Population: 32 million (2009 figures)

The mega city, also known as the ‘city of fog’, suffers from some of the worst air pollution in the world. Main culprits: coal burning by power plants and emissions by industry. Levels of nitrogen are also on the rise due to increased vehicle ownership. According to recent studies, 4.63% of children under 14 suffer from asthma.
