Population: 3. 5 million (2007 figures)

Taiyuan—at the center of China’s coal country—has consistently ranked among the world’s most polluted cities. Taiyuan’s air pollution woes are directly related to the endless demand for this area’s coal, iron and steel production. Grayish smog and white shirts turned black around the collar and sleeves are regular occurrences in this polluted city.
